1. Overview

NorthHorizonMails ("we," "our," "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our customers and website visitors. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

By using our website or services, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you have questions, please contact us at support@northhorizonmails.com.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

  • Account information: Name, email address, business name, and billing information when you place an order or contact us.
  • Order information: Domain names, sending platform details, inbox configuration preferences, and technical setup information you provide during onboarding.
  • Support communications: Emails, chat messages, or other communications you send to our support team.

Information collected automatically

  • Usage data: Pages visited, time spent, referring URLs, and general interaction data collected through standard web analytics.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, operating system, and device type collected automatically when you visit our website.
  • Cookies: See Section 5 for details.

Information we do NOT collect

We do not access, read, or store the content of emails sent through the inboxes we provision. Our role is infrastructure provisioning. The content of your email campaigns is your data and your responsibility.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provision and manage your inbox infrastructure
  • Process payments and send billing communications
  • Provide customer support and respond to your inquiries
  • Send transactional emails (order confirmations, setup updates, account notices)
  • Monitor for abuse or violations of our Acceptable Use Policy
  • Improve our services and website through analytics
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising targeting on third-party platforms.

4. Third-Party Services

We share limited information with the following third-party services to operate our business:

  • Stripe — Payment processing. Stripe handles all payment card data. We do not store raw payment card information. Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment data.
  • Microsoft Azure / Microsoft 365 — Infrastructure provider for the inboxes and tenants we provision. Microsoft's data processing terms apply to tenant infrastructure.
  • Intercom — Customer support chat. Your name and email may be shared with Intercom to facilitate support conversations.
  • Analytics providers — We may use privacy-respecting web analytics (such as Plausible or similar) to understand website traffic. These tools do not use cookies or collect personally identifiable information.

We require all third-party service providers to maintain appropriate security standards and to use your data only for the purposes of providing services to us.

5. Cookies

Our website uses a minimal set of cookies:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function (e.g., session management). These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies: Used to understand how visitors interact with our site. These are anonymized and do not track you across other websites.

We do not use advertising cookies, retargeting cookies, or cross-site tracking cookies. You can disable non-essential cookies through your browser settings.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services and comply with legal obligations.

  • Account and billing data: Retained for 7 years for accounting and legal compliance purposes.
  • Support communications: Retained for 3 years after your last interaction.
  • Technical logs: Retained for 90 days.

After the applicable retention period, data is deleted or anonymized. You may request earlier deletion subject to Section 7.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Objection: Object to certain processing activities.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at support@northhorizonmails.com with the subject line "Privacy Request." We will respond within 30 days.

8. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encrypted transmission (HTTPS), access controls, and regular security reviews. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We encourage you to use strong, unique passwords and to contact us immediately if you suspect unauthorized access to your account.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for business users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 18. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by email or by posting a notice on our website. The "last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Your continued use of our services after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

For privacy-related questions, requests, or concerns:

Email: support@northhorizonmails.com

We aim to respond to all privacy inquiries within 5 business days.
